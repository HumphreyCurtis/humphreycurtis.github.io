About me

I am a third-year PhD candidate within the Department of Informatics at King's College London.

Member of the Human Centred Computing Research group .

I am supervised by Dr Timothy Neate and my second supervisor is Dr Rita Borgo.

My research focuses upon wearable, discreet augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) devices.

Outside of my research, I have a wide variety of interests. Particularly, I really enjoy sports, reading, travel and food. Currently, I am a long suffering Manchester United fan and have always been an avid golfer. My family has three dogs (pictured) which means plenty of walking. If I am not frantically typing into a computer you'll most probably find me enjoying a cup of coffee or a good pint of bitter!
