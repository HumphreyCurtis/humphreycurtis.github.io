** Supplementary file descriptions 

* Advokit-Cards.pdf - The full accessible, tangible Advokit card-deck designed to support co-design engagements on UK digitised welfare systems. The deck comprises four sections: HELP cards outlining benefits and services available to disabled applicants; WHERE cards highlighting the technologies, resources, and locations through which welfare services are accessed; BARRIER cards identifying systemic challenges; and FEELINGS cards capturing co-designers’ emotional experiences.

* Toolkit-Difficulty-Value-Grid.pdf - A printable toolkit grid for ranking services (e.g., HELP cards) by perceived difficulty and value. Cards can be placed from Low → Very Important and Easy → Hard to support prioritisation discussions.

* Toolkit-Journey-Mapping.pdf - A printable toolkit of journey-mapping materials for charting service pathways and identifying hurdles encountered along the way. It can be supplemented with WHERE and BARRIER cards to enrich discussion.

* Video Prompt 1 - A Future of Care.mp4 - Produced by the design studio Superflux. Pam, a stroke survivor in her 60s, Nadia, a care professional in her 50s, and Yanni, a younger disabled tech worker, use AI technologies such as driverless cars, telepresence robots, and voice-activated smart homes to address practical and bureaucratic challenges.

* Video Prompt 2 - Press 1 to be Denied.mp4 - Eileen, 75, from Haringey, calls the National Mobility Support Line to request a Blue Badge.

* Video Prompt 3 - Your Eligibility Could Not be Confirmed.mp4 - Maurice, 73, from Stratford, is assessed for PIP over a video call with PIPA, an AI assistant.
 
* Video Prompt 4 - Just Want to See the Doctor.mp4 - Mark, 82, from Islington, struggles to book a GP appointment. After giving up on lengthy phone queues, the NHS app redirects him to a health questionnaire that denies service and recommends a pharmacist.

* Video Prompt 5 - We’ll Sort it Out Together, Brenda.mp4 - Brenda, 65, from Bromley, uses Homely, her Council Housing chatbot assistant, to request home adaptations including a handrail and lower light switch.
